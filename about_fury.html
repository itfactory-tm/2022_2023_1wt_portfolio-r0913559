About

Wim is a 40-year-old cycling enthusiast who has been riding his bike for as long as he can remember. He started competing in local races when he was just a teenager, and quickly worked his way up the ranks to become one of the top riders in his region. Over the years, Wim has competed in numerous national and international races, and has earned a reputation for his strong work ethic and strategic racing style. In his free time, Wim loves nothing more than hitting the open road on his bike and exploring new trails and routes. Despite his busy schedule, he always makes time to ride, whether it's a quick spin around the block or a long, grueling climb up a mountain pass.
